# Balancr — 1‑Minute Video Script

Opening (0–10s)
- Problem: balancing work, health, and play is hard. Fast motivation shots.
- Narration: “We made logging effortless, so balance becomes visible and actionable.”

Core Flow (10–35s)
- Show Sign Up and Add Activity (Variant B). Tap 25m preset, pick category, title, press Add Now.
- Overlay: “Under 30 seconds to log.”

Insight (35–50s)
- Dashboard pie chart appears. Narration: “See where time goes, at a glance.”
- Compare Variant A vs B briefly; note speed and ease.

Close (50–60s)
- Call to action: “Balance your day with Balancr.” Logo and URL.

Shot List
- Device close‑ups, over‑the‑shoulder, chart reveal, CTA.
