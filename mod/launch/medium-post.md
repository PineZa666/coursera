# Balancr — Balancing Work and Play, One Log at a Time

Summary
- Balancr helps you log time across categories in seconds and see balance at a glance.
- The design emphasizes momentum: quick presets, clear chart, and minimal friction.

User Need
- People want a simple way to maintain a healthy balance without heavy data entry.

Design Insight
- If logging takes under 30 seconds, users sustain the habit and gain insight. Presets and a strong CTA reduce cognitive load and time‑to‑submit.

Prototype Overview
- Mobile‑first app with Sign Up, Add Activity, Activities list, and Dashboard with a pie chart.
- A/B variants test the original flow vs. a preset‑based quick log.

Research & Testing
- In‑person sessions surfaced hesitation around minutes entry; presets resolved friction.
- Online A/B test measures conversions and time‑to‑submit between variants.

Iterations
- Reordered fields to match user mental models.
- Enhanced CTA and added 25/50/75 minute presets.
- Clarified legend and spacing on the chart.

Secret Sauce
- Fast logging through presets plus immediate visualization encourages daily use.

What We Learned
- Reducing choices speeds action without harming accuracy.
- Clear, glanceable visuals motivate behavior change.

Next Steps
- Edit/delete activities, time ranges (week/month), onboarding micro‑tour, accessibility improvements.
